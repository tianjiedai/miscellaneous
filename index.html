Fudan University is one of my dream schools. When I started my undergraduate studies in 2019, I visited Fudan University during the Mid-Autumn Festival. I witnessed the majesty of the Guanghua Building and felt the steadfast ideological beliefs of Chen Wangdao as he translated the Communist Manifesto. Last year, one of my high school classmates was admitted to the School of Data Science, Fudan University, and I revisited this scenery in December 2023 and April 2024. Wish her good luck with job hunting.

🎈

Beijing is the area with the highest concentration of higher education resources in China. I plan to visit Beijing this fall to tour various universities and reconnect with old classmates, including those from THU, PKU, RUC, UCAS, BUAA, BNU and BIT.

🎡

At the same time, Beijing also boasts a rich array of natural and cultural landscapes. Considering time constraints, I plan to travel by the benchmark model of the Beijing-Shanghai high-speed rail, and I am looking forward to experiencing CR400AF-Z.
