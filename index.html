Fudan University is one of my dream schools. When I started my undergraduate studies in 2019, I visited Fudan University during the Mid-Autumn Festival. I witnessed the majesty of the Guanghua Building and felt the steadfast ideological beliefs of Chen Wangdao as he translated the Communist Manifesto. Last year, one of my high school classmates was admitted to the School of Data Science, Fudan University, and I revisited this university on Dec. 9, 2023 and Apr. 27, 2024.

🏥

During the summer vacation of my first year as a graduate student, I went to Hefei and Nanjing to participate in social practice at national defense and military industry units. I visited iFlytek and the 14th, 28th, 38th, and 55th Research Institutes of China Electronics Technology Group Corporation. Besides the social practice, I visited a high school classmate who was interning in the oncology department at Jiangsu Provincial People's Hospital on Jul. 11, 2024. I gained a detailed understanding of the clinical value and practical significance of AI for Medicine, especially in the prevention and treatment of breast cancer, contributing to the well-being of all humanity.
